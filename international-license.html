How To Get International Driving License In Qatar 2024 ?

By Hassam AnsariUpdated : 11/01/2024

To get an internation driving licesne (IDP) in Qatar is very easy process.
Here's the steps to followed.

  • Eligibility :
    1. Hold a Qatari valid driving license.
    2. Be at least 18 years old.
    3. Have a valid Qatari residet permit with at least 6 months of remaing.
    4. Have a valid passport.
  • Required Documents :
    1. Application form provided by the issuing authority.
    2. Tow passport size of photographs.
    3. Copy of Qatari valid driving license.
    4. Copy of Qatar ID.
    5. Copy of Passport.
    6. Payment fee (usually QR 150).
  • Where to apply :
    1. Qatar Automobile and Touring Club(QATC) :

      2. This is the official authorized body for issuing IDPs in Qatar. Visit their office in Doha (Sat-Thu: 8am-12pm) with the required documents and fee.

    2. Authorized Driving Schools :

      3. Some driving schools are authorized to issue IDPs, like Karwa Driving School.

    3. Travel Agency :

      4. Certain travel agencies, like Mannai Air Travel, also offer IDP services.

  • Online Providers :

    • Organizations like ITCA offer online applications for IDPs. While processing might be faster (15 minutes), they only provide a digital copy. You'll need to arrange separate printing and delivery, and ensure the provider is recognized in your intended destination country.

  • Additional Tips :
    • The IDP Valid for one year from date of issue.
    • Always carry original qatar driving license while driving abroad.
    • check the traffic regulations and driving requirements of the countries you plan to visit before your trip.
