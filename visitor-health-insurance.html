How To Get Visitors Health Insurance In Qatar ?

By Dohafacts.com / updated : 24/01/2024

As of February 1, 2023. you can't enter the country without valid insurance. Visitor's health insurance is mandatory in Qatar.

Let's explore the procedures how to get visitors health insurance in Qatar :

Important things to remember :

  • Manadotry Requirement :

    • Visitor's health insurance is manadotry in Qatar as of February 1, 2023. You can't enter without health insurance in Qatar.

  • Coverage :

    • All plan must cover emergency medical expenses and accidents with minimum of QR 50,000/-. you can opt for more comprehensive plans with additionals coverage.

  • Policy Duration :

    • The insurance validity corresponds to your visa duration. you can exted insurance if your visa is extended.

  • Documents :

    • Carry copies of your passport, visa, and insurance policy document during your stay in Qatar.

  • Familiarize Yourself :

    • Read through the policy term and conditions to understand covered services, network hospitals, and claims procedures.

  • In case of medical emergency :

    • Contact your insurance provider for quidance on claiming you benefits.

There are Three main ways to get health insurance in Qatar.

  1. Online :

    2. This is the most easiest way to obtain health insruance in Qatar. Popular Provider like QLM and QIC offer online purchase options on their webistes :

    • QLM : https://qlm.com.qa/visitors-mandatory-health-insurance/
    • QIC : https://qic.online/en/visitors

    Here's how to buy insurance online :

    • Choose Your Plan :

      • Both QLM and QIC offer different plans with varying coverage amounts and additonal benefits. Compare and choose the one that suits your needs and budget.

    • Enter Your Information :

      • You'll nedd your passport details, visa information and travel dates.

    • Select You Payment Method :

      • Pay with your debit card or credit card.

    • Download Your Policy Document :

      • Once payment is processed, you'll receive a downloadable policy document containing your coverage details and network hospitals.

  2. Metrash2 App :

    3. Download the Metrash2 app and purchase your insurance directly through it.

    • Open the app and navigate to the Visitors Health Insurance section.
    • Follow the instructions, submit your details, choose a plan and pay online.
    • Download your policy document for your records.
  3. Upon Arrival :

    4. Kiosks are available at Hamad International Airport and border entry points.

    • Follow the on-screen instructions at the kiosk, provid your information, and select your plan.
    • Pay using the available payment methods ( usually debit / credit card) and collect your policy document.

By following these steps you can easily obtain the neccessary health insurance for your visit to Qatar.
