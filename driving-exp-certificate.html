How To Get Qatar Driving License Certificate in 2024?

By Dohafacts.com / updated : 11/01/2024

Qatar Minstry of Interior (MOI) has an online service that allows Qatar residents to submit a request for getting a driving liceense certificate. To get a driving license certificate residents should have valid Qatar driving license.

Let's look at the following steps to how to obtain license certificate :

There are two ways you can get driving certificate.

  1. Using Metrash2 Mobile App.
  2. And Using MOI e-Sercvices Portal.

How To Get It Using Metrash2 Mobile App ?

  1. The first Step is to download Metrash2 app from google play store or IOS App store.
  2. Register to Metrash2 Mobile App.
  3. Lon in to Metrash2 Mobile app using your QID and Mobile Number.
  4. Next Click on the Traffic icon.
  5. Next step is to click on the Traffic certificate icon under the Traffic service.
  6. Next, click on the Driving license certificate.
  7. Next, enter your email ID where you will get your certificate.
  8. pay the fee (QAR 10) and the certificate will sent to your given email ID.

How To get through MOI portal ?

  1. First step is to log in with your smartcard to portal. Click Here To Open MOI Portal.
  2. Click On the Traffic service icon
  3. Next, step go to Requests and Certificate.
  4. Next, select the Traffic certificate and the Driving License Certificate.
  5. Next, page enter your email ID.
  6. And pay the required fee that is (QAR 10).
