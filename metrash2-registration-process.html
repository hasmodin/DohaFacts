A Detail Guides About Metrash2 Mobile Application.

By Hassam AnsariUpdated : 14/01/2024

In this article, you'll see step by step user guide about metrash2 mobile application.

Metrash2 is a updated version of metrash mobile application created by the Ministry of Interior(MOI) in Qatar.
It allows residents and citizens to access over 100 Government online services easly from their smartphone.
Wiht metrash2 you can :

  • Apply for and renew visas and residence permit
  • Pay traffic fines and others government feees
  • Check your immigration and travel history
  • Update your personal information
  • Reporst emergencies
  • And much more !

Here's is detailed guide to using metrash2 in Doha Qatar.

  1. Download and Install the app :

    2. Metrash2 app is available on both the ISO App Store and Google Play Store. Simply download and install to your smartphone.

  2. Register or Activate :

    3. If you're a new user, you need to register with Qatar ID number, expiry date, and mobile number.
    you'll receive an activation code via SMS, Which you'll need to enter to complete the registration process.

    If you've already used metrash2 before, you can simply activate it on your new device by entring your QID number & password.
    You'll receive OTP(One Time Password)on your registered device, which you'll neet to enter to activate metrash2 on your new phone.

  3. Expolore the services :

    4. The metrash2 app interface is user-friendly and easy to navigate.
    The home screen displays a variety of commonly used services, such as visa application, traffic fine payment, and address update.

    You can also search for specific services using the search bar.

  4. Use the Services :

    5. Once you've selected a service, you'll be guided through a step by step process to complete your request.
    This may involve filling out forms, uploading documents, and making payments.
    The app provides clear instructions and helpful tips throughout the process.

  5. Track your request :

    6. You can track the status of your requests within the app.
    Simply go to the My Request section to see a list of your pending and completed transations.

  6. Additional Features :
    • New and announcements : Stay updated on the latest news and announcements from the MOI.
    • Notifiactions : Receive alerts about important information and updates related to your government services.
    • Feedback : Provide feedback on your experience using Metrash2.
  • Benefits of Using Metrash2 :
    1. convenience : Access government services anytime, anywhere from your mobile phone.
    2. Time Saving : No need to wait in line at government offices.
    3. Efficiency : Complete transactions quickly and easly.
    4. Tranparency : Track the status of your requests and stay informed about important update.

Overall, Metrash2 is a valuable tool for anyone living in Doha, Qatar.
It makes accessing government services easier, faster and more convenient.
