How To Apply For ETA Visa Online 2024 ?

By Hassam AnsariUpdated : 14/01/2024

Here's a guide on how to apply for ETA visa online for Qatar.

Applying for an ETA visa in Qatar is a simple and straightforward process that can be done entirely online.

Follow the below steps :

  1. Check if you need an ETA visa :

    2. Not all nationality require an ETA visa for entry into Qatar. You can check the list of visa-exempt countries on the official Qatar airways website.

    If your country is not on the list, you'll need to apply for an ETA visa.

  2. Gather the required documents :
    • A valid passport with at least Six moths of validity.
    • A recent digital passport size photo in JPEG format.
    • A valid Email address.
    • A credit card or debit card for payment.
  3. Apply Online :

    4. There are two official webiste to apply ETA visa Online.

      • Minsitry of Interior(MOI) https://portal.moi.gov.qa/wps/portal/MOIInternet/MOIHome
      • Hayya Portal https://www.hayya.qa/

        • Both website offers similar type of application form.

  4. Wait for a decision :

    5. You'll receive a email within 48 hours wheather your ETA visa application has been approved or denied.

  5. Print your ETA visa :

    6. Once your ETA visa application has been approved, then you are able to download and print the visa.
    Must present your ETA visa and passport on arrvial of Qatar airport.

Additonal Tips to apply ETA visa :

  • Make sure you have stable internet connection while applying online.
  • Make sure that all the information you enter is correct befor submitting the application.
  • Keep your printed ETA visa and passport safe util you arrive Qatar.

Helpful Links :
