How To Book QDC Appointment Online ?

By Hassam AnsariUpdated : 14/01/2024

Here's a guide on how to book QDC appointment online in Qatar.

  1. Navigate to the QDC website :

    2. Visit the official QDC website . https://www.qdc.com.qa/

  2. Choose your appointment type :

    3. Click on the Appointments tab at the top of the page. you'll see different appointment options:

      • Store visit : For shopping at a QDC retail store.
      • Permit renewal/Lost Card : For renewing your existing permit or replacing a lost one.
      • New permit/Joint Permit/Review Office : For applying for a new permit, applying for a joint with your spouse, or visiting the review office.
  3. Provide Your Details :

    4. Enter Your :

      • Permit Number(if applicable)
      • Qatar ID number
      • Email Address
      • Mobile Number
      • Captcha code displayed on the screen
  4. Select your preferred date and time :

    5. The website will display available appointment slot based on your chosen type. Select the most convenient date and time for you.

  5. Confirm and receive confirmation :

    6. Review the appointment details and click Cofirm to complete the booking. You'll receive a confirmation email and or sms with your appointment details.

Additional Tips :

1. Book your appointment well in advance, as slots can fill up quickly.

2. Ensure you have all the required documents needed for your chosen appointment type.

3. You can reschedule or cancel your appointment online.

4. If you have any issues booking online, you can contact QDC directly through their webiste or by calling their customer service number.
