Conditions For Bringing Families On Visit Visa In Qatar.

By Hassam Ansari Updated : 10/01/2024

General Conditons :

  • 1. The applying resident should have family accommodation duly accredited by relevant authorities.
  • 2. The applying resident should possess a minimum monthly salary of QAR 5000
  • 3. The profession of the applying residen should be in the non-labour category.
  • 4. The visitor must possess health insurance for the period of their visit of the State of Qatar
  • 5. The visitor must be a relative of the applying resident within the allowed degrees of relation.

Application must be submitted through the Metrash2 mobile app
