Conditions For Bringing Families For Residence Visa In Qatar.

By Hassam Ansari Updated : 10/01/2024

General Conditons :

  • 1. The Son Should not be more than 25 years of age
  • 2. The daughter must be Unmarried.
  • 3. Children with Compulsary eduacation age(6-18) must be enrolled in schooling inside or outside the country.
  • 4. The expatriates being hosted should have health insurance for the duration of thier stay, starting from thedate of entry into the country.

For government and Semi-Government Employees :

  • 1. The employee should have family housing allocated by the employer or a minimum monthly salary of QAR 10,000 duly accredited with an employemnt contract.

For Private Sector Employees:

  • 1. The employee's profession should be of a technical or specialized (non-labour) Category, accredited with an employement contract.
  • 2. The employee should have a minimum salary of QAR 10,000 or QAR 6,000, with family housing provide by the employer and duly accredited with an employement contract.

Application must be submitted through the Metrash2 mobile app
