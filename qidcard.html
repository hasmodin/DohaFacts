What Is Your Qatar ID Card Number Meaning ?

By Dohafacts.com / updated : 14/01/2024

Qatar Government use national Id card to track their citizens and residents for purposes of work, health care and government benifits.

This Article tell you everything you need to know about your Qatar ID card Number :

What is Qatar ID Number ?

Qatar ID number is an 11-digit identification number issued by the government of Qatar to its citizens and residents for the purpose of residency,
employemt, health care, and other government related works.

How To Know Qatar ID Number

A new resident can know his Qatar ID numbe when his Qatar Id card is issued. The Qatar ID number would be given on front side of his ID card.

Qatar Id number meaning and format

Qatar ID number is not randomly generated but includes important inforation such as your birth year and nationality?
A Qatar ID split into 4 parts and here is what each part means :

  1. The first digit indicate the Century of your birth.
    Resident born between 1900 and 1999 will have the first digit as 2;
    Resident born after 2000 will have the first digit as 3.
  2. The following two digit are your year of birth(YOB). Like for someone born in 1988 the last two digit would be 88.
  3. The Next Three digits show you Nationality by ISO 3166 Country Code. Each country has an different ISO code. it is 524 for Nepal.
  4. The last five digits show the sequence number for a combination of your birth year and ISO country code.
    This will be unique for each person and is generated in the order of your residency.
