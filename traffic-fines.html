Qatar Traffic Violation Fines And Points.

By Hassam AnsariUpdated : 11/01/2024

The specific fines and point associated with a Traffic violation in Qatar will depend on the nature of the offense.
Here's general overview.

  1. fines :
    • Speeding :

      • Fines start at QR 500 and increase by QR 100 for every 10km/h over the posted limit, up to a maximum of QR 10,000.

    • Red Light Running :

      • Breaking a red signal in Qatar incurs a hefty penalty of QR 6000, along with 7 demerit points on your driver's license.

    • Mobile Phone Use While Driving :

      • While Driving use of Mobile gives you QR 500 but do not qualify for a discount.

    • Not Wearing Seatbelt :

      • The fine for Not Wearing Seatbelt is QR 500 and you can get discount if you pay it within a moth.

    • Driving without a license :

      • QR 10,000 and confiscation of the vehicle.

  1. Points
    • Each traffic violation carrries a certain number of demerit points, ranging from 1 to 7.
    • Getting too many points can lead to license suspension:
      • 14 points in the first time - 3 months suspension
      • 12 points in the second time - 6 months suspension
      • 10 points in the third time - 9 months suspension
      • 8 points in the fourth time - 1 year suspension
