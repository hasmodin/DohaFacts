How To Convert GCC Driving License To Qatar Driving License 2024 ?

By Dohafacts.com / updated : 11/01/2024

To converting GCC Driving License to Qatar driving licens is easy process.
Here's some steps to follow.

  1. Visit a Driving School :

    2. Visit any driving school in Qatar.These School typically have a Department of Traffice (DTO) offie withing them.

    If your are unsure about driving school in Qatar Please Visit the MOI Website for the authorized school.

  2. Open File With a Department of Traffic :
    • In Doha Qatar inside every school you will find DOT. Inform them you want to convert your GCC driving license.
    • Provide them following documents
      • Original GCC driving license, must be valid
      • Qatar Resident Permit QID.
      • Eye Test certificate(tested from any approved government clinic).
      • QR 50 For opening the file(payable by debit or credit card)
  3. Theory Class and Test :
    • You may need to enroll in a short theory class depending on your GCC license and the driving school's requirements.
    • Book a date for theory test after completing the class (if applicable).
    • Pass the theory test to proced forther.
  4. Road Test and Parking Test :
    • Pay the road test fees QR 250 and book the test date.
    • Before the road test, you may need to complete a practical driving session at the driving school (again depend on your GCC license and School requirements).
    • Take and pass the road test and parking test successfully.
  5. Pay License Fee & Collect :
    • Once you pass the all tests, pay the license fee QR 250 .
    • Collect your new driving license.
  • Additional Tips

    • Visit the DoT office early mornning 6:30 am to 9: am to avoid long wait times.

    Ensure your GCC license is valid and from recognized GCC country.

    Check the official Ministry of interior website or contact the DoT directly for any updates or changes to the process.

Remember, these are general information, and specific requirements might differ slightly depending on your GCC country
and individual circumstances.
