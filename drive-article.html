Qatar Driving License Procedure With Easy Steps 2024.

By Hassam Ansari Updated : 10/01/2024

Getting a Driving License can be a very easy process, but it's important to follow the steps carefully and make sure you have all the required documentation. Here's a breakdown of the process:

  1. Obatian a No Objection Certificate(NOC) :
    • This is required if you're sponsored by an employer in Qatar. You can request it through the Metrash2 app or the Ministry of Interior (MOI) website.
  2. Choose a driving school :
    • There are many driving schools in Qatar, so research and compare prices and schedules before making a decision.
  3. Complete an eye test :
    • This can be done at most driving schools or at authorized optometrists.
  4. Apply for a Driving License :
    • You can do this online through the MOI website or at any driving school. You'll need your passport , QID, eye test certificate, and NOC (if applicable).
  5. Choose a course :
    • If you don't already have a valid driver's license from another country, you'll need to take a driving course.
  6. Take the theory test :
    • This covers traffic rules and regulations in Qatar. You can study the official MOI driving manual or take practice tests online.
  7. Pass the practical driving test :
    • This will assess your driving skills on a closed course and on the road.
  8. Collect your driver's license :
    • Once you pass all the tests, you can collect your driver's license from the Traffic Department.

Here are some additional things to keep in mind :

  • The minimum age to get driving license in qatar is 18 years old.
  • The cost of getting a driver's license can vary depending on the driving schools and the type of course you choose.
  • You can renew your Qatar driver's licens through MOI website and Metrash2 Mobile App.
