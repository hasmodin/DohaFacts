Qatar Driving License Procedure With Easy Steps 2024.

By Hassam Ansari

You should have a Qatar driving license to drive motor vehicles legally on roads. To get a driving license, your minimum age for light motor vehicles is 18 years, and 21 years for heavy vehicles.

Procedure To Get Qatar Driving License.

In Qatar, the administration has streamlined the process for individuals looking to convert their GCC driving licenses into a Qatar driving license, obtain an international driving license, or renew existing Qatar Driving license. If you don’t possess any of these documents, you can also apply for a new driving license by following the guidelines provided in this article. This efficient system accommodates both Qataris and non-Qataris, making the process accessible and straightforward for all

First, you need to practice, so get yourself enrolled in any government-authorized driving school and obtain a learner permit. Learn, Practice, and pass the driving exam. Typically, You have to pass the Theory test, Road test, and parking test (L & P) to obtain a drivers license.

There is a standard rule for a driving license the world follows. The traffic department is administered by the Ministry of Interior’s (MOI) General Directorate of Traffic. Several authorized driving schools for new aspirants follow the same curriculum nationwide.

Guidelines For Qatari Nationals Applying For A Driving License

  • 1. The minimum age is 18 for light vehicles and 21 for heavy vehicles.
  • 2. Aspirant should have a Valid Residence Permit (QID)
  • 3. A Visa Copy and original passport are required in case QID is not issued.
  • 4. Enrol in authorized driving school.
  • 5. Colour Photographs (3x Passport Size)
  • 6. Advance 3 Months Driving learning deposit if you wish to learn using a private car.

Guidelines For Non-Qataris Seeking a Driving License in Qatar.

  • 1. The minimum age is 18.
  • 2. ID Card and its 1 Copy.
  • 3. A Visa Copy and original passport are required in case QID is not issued.
  • 4. A copy of the sponsor ID card ( if the sponsor is a person).
  • 5. Approval Letter from the sponsor to approve the request for a new license.
  • 6. Colour Photographs (3x Passport Size).

Guidelines for diplomats and officials of international missions for Qatar Driving License:

  • 1. Book of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs
  • 2. A copy of the diplomatic card
  • 3. Original license
  • 4. 3 recent colour photographs (passport size)

Following are the steps to complete driving license application:

  • 4. Decide Course Duration
  • 5. Submit Course Duration

Step 1: Choose Driving School

Qatar is a country with an eclectic mix of technologies and facilities. Qatar Driving schools are equipped with the latest vehicles like Audi, Mercedes, etc according to the needs of aspirants. Interestingly, they all follow the same curriculum which is provided by MOI.

Step 2: Apply for NOC (No Objection Certificate)

As Qatar is shifting towards technology, applicants can easily get NOC using an e-portal by MOI or Metrash mobile application online. There are some requirements to follow:

  • 1. NOC is not required for Qatari males.
  • 2. NOC has to be given by a guardian for Qatari females.
  • 3. NOC has to be given to expatriates from sponsorship companies.

Step 3: Eye Test ( Vision Test)

The eye test is mandatory for a driving license, Some driving schools have eye-test (vision test) facilities in their schools which update the result on the MOI website if you test your eyes. Otherwise, you can bring your eye test from a recognized ophthalmologist or optometrist from the traffic department. There is a fee for this test that varies between QR 25 to QR 50.

Step 4: Choose a Course for a Driving License

There are so many authorized driving schools that offer a range of courses on different vehicles with both male and female faculty. If you are a foreigner and you have driving experience you may get half a course after approval from the Traffic department. In this case, you need to submit your foreign driver’s license and they will advise you suitable course.

Step 5: Submit Documents and Pay the Fee

The traffic department needs some documents to verify your identity, The following is the list of documents that are required at the time of registration:

  • 1. Valid Qatar ID at least 18 years old
  • 2. NOC from Sponsor ( Metrash2 app)
  • 3. Recent Colour passport size photographs. ( If you are an eyeglass wearer then take a photo in spectacles)
  • 4. Eye test (Vision test) certificate
  • 5. Original Previous country driver’s license if you are applying for half course
  • 6. Pay the Fee

After submitting all the documents and fees. Driving school fees vary from QR 250 to QR 600. Your driving school will open an account where you would learn theory in the form of a PDF or CD and some good schools have their own mobile applications and website to teach you in a better way.

Step 6: Theory Lesson & Test for Drivers License

From this step, you start learning and progressing. First, you learn about theory lessons which include traffic rules, traffic signs driving ethics, and much more. You will be provided with a tablet or computer in class where you will learn and test your progress accordingly. Some schools have made software for aspirants to test themselves and for a better experience, and few schools offer simulator training as well for better steering control.

After the theory lessons, you can appear in the theory test. It is simply based on 40 questions and it is necessary to answer 37 correct answers to pass this test. For the ease of aspirants, this test is available in multiple languages and it is conducted online nowadays

Step 7: Practical Lessons for Qatar Driving License

After the completion of the theory test, your training starts with the driving instructor where you will learn in real-time experience by driving a vehicle on the roads. This is the main part of your course. You can choose your driving instructor on your own. If you need a female instructor you may charge more.

This session will help you learn the basic techniques for the use of indicators, brakes, and accelerators. You need to master “P” and “L”, that is the core of this task.

Step 8: Road & Parking Test

When you are confident about your skills you will be given a date for the road test. In which, a traffic officer and 2 other people will sit with you in a car and they will observe your driving school. Upon the completion of the road test, you will be given a date for the parking test.

In the parking test, You need to pass the “L” and “P”. These tasks need to be passed on the same day, P means Parallel parking and L means perpendicular parking. Once you successfully pass this test, You will be awarded a driving license immediately.

Qatar is a small, wealthy country located in the Persian Gulf region, known for its modern and innovative architecture, including the iconic Burj Qatar and the Museum of Islamic Art.
