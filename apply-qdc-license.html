How To Apply for QDC License In Qatar ?

By Hassam AnsariUpdated : 14/01/2024

Here's a guide on how to apply for a QDC license Liquor Pertmit in Qatar.

Eligiblity :

  • Non GCC citizens with a valid Qatar Resident Permit(RP).
  • Over 21 Years Old.
  • Basic Salary (Excluding allowences) of at least QAR 3,000 per month.
  • Private accommodation(not applicable to those living in hotels.

Application Process :

  1. Book an online appointment :
    • Visit the QDC website : https://www.qdc.com.qa/
    • Click on "New Permit" and schedule an appointment.
  2. Gather a required documents :
    • Original Qatar ID passport with RP.
    • Letter from your employer in english addressed to QDC , stating :

      • Name


      position


      Basic Salary Breakdown


      accommodation entitlement(Free housing or allowence)


      Signed and stamped by an authorized signatroy


      Dated with at least 3 months


      On Original company letterhead

  3. Visit the QDC office :
      • Attend you appointment at the QDC office in Abu Hamour(location may change, check the website).
      • Bring the required document and pay the application fee (QAR 150 for 12 months, QAR 175 for 15 months and QAR 192 for 17 months).
      • Your photo will be taken and you'll be issued a temproray permit.
  4. Collect your license :
      • The official license will be ready for collection within a few days. you'll be notified via SMS.

Additional Tips :

    • Renewal : License can be renew online or in person.
    • Click & collect : Pre-order products online and collect them at a specific time.
    • Spouse application : Both spouse can apply for a joint permit.
    • Pork prodcut : Sold to non Musalim only.
    • Alcohol consumption : Only allowed at the permit holder's resident.
    • Permit holder and goods : Must be in the same vehicle during transportation.

For more inforation please do visit QDC official website.
