How To Check Qatar Visa Online And Printing Guide 2024.

By Hassam AnsariUpdated : 22/01/2024

Here's a guide on How to check and print your Qatar visa online :

Checking your visa status :

  1. Visit the Ministry of Interior(MOI) website : https://portal.moi.gov.qa/wps/portal/MOIInternet/MOIHome
  2. Select Visa Services From the menu.
  3. Choose Visa Inquiry and Printing.
  4. You can select to enter your passport number or visa number.
  5. Enter your nationality and the captcha code displayed on the screen.
  6. Click Submit.
  7. Your visa status you'll be displayed, including validity, purpose, and entry/exit dates.

Printing your visa :

  1. If your visa is approved, you can click print visa on the confirmation page.
  2. Ensure you have a printer connected and your paper is ready.
  3. The visa will be downloaded as a PDF document.
  4. Open the PDF and print it.
  5. You may need to sign the printout in the designated space.

    6. Additional Tips :

    • Save the PDF printout for your records.
    • You can also check you visa status through the MOI's Smartphone app Metrash2.
    • If you encounter any difficulties, you can contact the MOI call center at +974 4444 8888

    Important Note :

    It's crucial to remember that this information is for informational purposes only and official procedures may chenge.

    The most updated information is always available on the MOI website.
