Hayya Visa Entry Into Qatar Is Closes.

By Dohafacts.com / updated : 12/02/2024

Doha, Qatar : The deadline for entry under the Hayya visa scheme for fans of the FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 came to a close on February 10.
Initially extended in December last year, the Hayya visa aimed to streamline the arrival of global fans and visitors attending Qatar's major sporting events and others activities.

The Hayya visa holders have started receiving email from Hayya Platform regarding the expiration of their Hayya visa entry,
they must return with a new Entry visa. "you can submit a new Hayya application throught the Hayya to Qatar App or on Hayya," the email said.

The Hayya Visa will remain valid until february 24 2024. According to the Ministry of Interior the last date of hayya holders to leave the country is 24 feb.
