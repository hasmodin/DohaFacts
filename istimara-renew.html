Renewing your Istimara (vehicle registration certificate) (2024) Online:

By Hassam AnsariUpdated : 10/01/2024

There are two main documents relevant to Qatar driver's : The Driving license and the Istimara (vehicle registration certificate).
You might be referring to either when you say road permit.
Here's how to renew each in 2024.

Renewing your Qatar Driving License in 2024 Online.

Please Click Here to go to the article.

Renewing Istimara (Road permit) Follow the below stpes.

    • e-Services by MOI:
  1. Visit MOI Website Link.
  2. Login with your Qatar ID details and PIN
  3. Click the traffic service and the "Istimara renewal"
  4. Follow the prompts and pay the fees online using a debit card or credit card
  5. Your renew Istimara will be delivered to registered address withing few working days.
    • Metrash2 Mobile App:
  1. Download the Metrash2 app from the App Store or Google Play
  2. Login with your QID details and PIN
  3. Click the traffic service and the "Istimara renewal"
  4. Follow the prompts and pay the fees online using a debit card or credit card
  5. Your renew Istimara will be delivered to registered address withing few working days.
    • In person:
  1. Visit the registration counter at any Woqod petrol station.
  2. Present your Istimara, vehicle insurance, and a valid Qatar ID.
  3. Pay the renewal fee with cash or credit card.
  4. Your renewed Istimara will be printed and ready for collection immediately.

Important points to remember:

  1. You can renew your Driving License up to Six months before its expiry date.
  2. You can renew your Istimara up to 60 days before its expiry date.
  3. Late renewal fees apply for both documents.
  4. Make sure you have all the necessary documents before diving into renewal process.
